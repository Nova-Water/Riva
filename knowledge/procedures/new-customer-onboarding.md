# Procedure: New Customer Onboarding

1. Record the customer's business name, contact person, phone, and email.
2. Identify which Nova Tech services the customer is interested in
   (IT support, CCTV, networking, website, software, cybersecurity).
3. Schedule an initial site visit or remote consultation.
4. Prepare a quotation covering scope, timeline, and cost (draft only —
   quotations require staff review before being sent to the customer).
5. Once approved internally, send the quotation to the customer.
6. On acceptance, create a project record in NovaCore and schedule work.

RIVA may help prepare drafts (documents, quotations, or emails) for steps 4–5,
but every draft requires human review and explicit confirmation before it is
sent or finalised.
