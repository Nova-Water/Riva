# Procedure: CCTV Installation Checklist

1. Confirm site address and access arrangements with the customer.
2. Walk the site to identify camera placement, coverage areas, and blind spots.
3. Confirm power and network cabling requirements.
4. Confirm recording/storage requirements (local NVR vs. cloud retention).
5. Install cameras, cabling, and the recording device.
6. Configure remote viewing access for the customer.
7. Test all cameras for coverage, night vision, and recording reliability.
8. Provide the customer with a handover document covering system use and
   support contact details.
9. Schedule a 30-day follow-up check.

RIVA can help search for related quotations, prior site notes, or generate a
handover document draft, but cannot schedule technician time or confirm
completion of physical work — those require a technician's direct input.
