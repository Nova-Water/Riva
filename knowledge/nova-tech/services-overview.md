# Nova Tech Ltd — Services Overview

## IT Support
On-site and remote support for desktops, laptops, servers, and small office
networks. Includes hardware diagnostics, software troubleshooting, and
preventative maintenance plans.

## CCTV Security Systems
Design and installation of CCTV camera systems for retail stores, offices,
warehouses, and residential properties. Includes remote viewing setup and
footage retention planning.

## Networking
Structured cabling, Wi-Fi design, router/firewall configuration, and
network security hardening for small and medium businesses.

## Websites
Business websites, landing pages, and simple e-commerce sites, built with
modern, maintainable technology and handed over with clear documentation.

## Software Systems
Nova Tech develops and maintains internal business software including
MYSKA Pay (payroll) and NovaCore (sales, inventory, and invoicing).

## Cybersecurity
Security assessments, staff awareness training, endpoint protection
recommendations, and incident response guidance.
