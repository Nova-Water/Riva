# About Nova Tech Ltd

Nova Tech Ltd is a Samoan-owned technology company based in Apia, Samoa. We provide
IT support, computer consultation and repair, CCTV security systems, networking,
websites, software systems, cybersecurity and business technology services to
businesses across Samoa.

## Mission

To empower Samoan businesses with reliable, professional, and locally-supported
technology solutions.

## Core Services

- IT support and helpdesk services for small and medium businesses
- Computer consultation, diagnostics, and repair
- CCTV security system design, installation, and maintenance
- Business and home networking (wired and wireless)
- Website design and development
- Custom software systems (including MYSKA Pay and NovaCore)
- Cybersecurity assessments and protection
- General business technology consulting
